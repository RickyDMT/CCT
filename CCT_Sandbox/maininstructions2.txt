The Gain Card: For every gain card you turn over, your score increases by either 10 or 30 points in different rounds. \n\n\nThe Loss Card: For every loss card you turn over, your score decreases by either 250 or 750 points, depending on the round. Furthermore, the round immediately ends (you cannot turn over any more cards). There will be either 1 or 3 loss cards in different rounds. \n\n\n The number of loss cards and the value of points that can be won or lost by turning over a gain or loss card are fixed in each round. This information will always be on display so you know what kind of round you are in
